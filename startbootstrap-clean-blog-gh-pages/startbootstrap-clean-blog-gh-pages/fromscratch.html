Here are some of my favorite ways to find new music on Spotify


Discover Weekly

Spotify's Discover Weekly playlist is a personalized playlist that is updated every Monday with new song suggestions, based on your listening history.


Release Radar

Spotify's Release Radar playlist includes new releases from artists you follow, as well as new songs from artists that Spotify thinks you might like.


For You Playlists

Spotify's "For You" playlists are personalized playlists for every mood. They include a mix of songs that you have listened to, as well as new songs that match the vibe of the playlist.
